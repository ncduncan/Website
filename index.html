Who We Are

West Neck Holdings was founded by Nat Duncan to acquire a family-owned small business in New England. We are the anti-private equity shop.

Born and raised in Connecticut, my family has lived in New England and run family businesses for multiple generations. I deeply appreciate the work ethic and values that drive this business community.

My career at General Electric spanned executive leadership roles in 20 cities across 4 continents before I returned to Boston where my wife Caitlin is a pediatrician at Boston Children's Hospital. We are committed to building our family here.

Outside work, I enjoy staying active by hiking, skiing, and sailing. I also enjoy traveling across New England with my wife to experience the region's history and culture. These connections shape my approach to business ownership and community impact.
